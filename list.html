My Summer 2023

Canes Takeaways

  • Food portions were good and still very hot considering the amount of people waiting
  • Dining area could have been a little bit bigger
  • Chicken was nice and crunchy and even better with their signiture Canes® sauce
  • Fries were also good and well saltened
  • Their bread, their bread was so soft and well cooked it was the best bread I have ever tasted and even more better with the Canes® Chicken and fried in there.
  • Lastly, one con is the sauce quantity, I think they could've given us a little more sauce.

But overall Canes® was a good first time experience, from the food presentation to the time it took to recieve the food. I would give it a 9.5 out of 10 would definitley recommened
